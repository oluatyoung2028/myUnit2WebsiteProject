Oasis Vibe

Oasis vibe is described as a blend of prickly pear (which has notes of kiwi, watermelon, and bubblegum) and subtle hints of lime. Generally speaking, it also has a tropical and sweet flavor. Overall, I don't have any complaints about this flavor, but it is harder to find in majority of stores. I give it a 9.89 out of 10.
