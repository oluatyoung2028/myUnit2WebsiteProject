Arctic Vibe

Arctic vibe is a blue rasberry flavor that is supposed to be a chill berry flavor. I personally like it, but it is very sweet and may not be to everyones liking. I have a major sweeth tooth so I do not mind the sweetest level being so high, but I'm aware not everyone shares that liking. Overall, I rate it a 8.93 out of 10.
